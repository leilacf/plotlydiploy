In conclusion: How and Why Should You Clean Your Belly Button?

The belly button can harbor hundreds of bacterial species, leading to a variety of health implications. One of the most prevalent issues is the development of yeast infections. Due to this area being dark and moist, with improper cleaning techniques, yeast infections can cause great discomfort and pain. To ensure a clean environment, it is reccomended to use a cotton swab with rubbing alcohol to gently rub the surface and to dry it with a towel before getting dressed. Aim to reduce sweat buildup as this can lead to increased omphaloliths--dead skin cells and sebum which when oxidized, can materialize as naval stones and secrete odor.
